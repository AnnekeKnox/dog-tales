Services on Offer

SERVICE DETAILS
Day and/or Night Care We provide care and love for your furbabies while you are at work or going away. With a massive, enclosed outdoor space, your furbabies are free to mingle and play with others whilst under supervision.
Grooming We provide high-quality grooming services, whether it be just a nice soak and wash or a nice shave or cut.
Food and Treats We provide home-made food and treats with only the best organic ingredients.
Toys We provide a wide range of toys made from safe materials that will provide hours of enrichment and entertainment for your furbaby.
Veterinary Services We have a resident Veterinerian on-site who is available 24/7 to make sure your pet is in the best care.
Clothing We have a wide range of jerseys to keep your furbaby warm in style!
Beds We provide dog beds tailored to your furbaby's needs, whether it be a fully-structured bed, kennel or a nice, comfy plush cushion.
Behaviour Therapy We have an on-site Animal Behaviouralist to provide you with professional guidance and tips on dealing with behavioural issues or general training.
Dog Walking We have dedicated dog walkers to walk your furbabies during the day.
Transport Services We provide local and international travel services, as well as a Pick-Up and Drop-Off service.
